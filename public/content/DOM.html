The Dominican Republic, like Jamaica, has a refinery which Venezuela control 49% of.

Also like Jamaica, the Dominican Republic was able to issue bonds in 2015 to pay off all of its Petrocaribe debt at half the price, thanks to Venezuela's urgent need for cash.

The D.R. is yet another country that benefits from Venezuelan largesse while not making many political concessions. Dominican officials once angered Venezuela by meeting with Venezuelan opposition leaders during the same trip during which they attended Petrocaribe meetings.
